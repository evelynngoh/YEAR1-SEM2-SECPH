Appreciation of Ethics and Civilizations course provided valuable understanding of ethics, civilization, and social harmony from various cultural and societal perspectives. Throughout the course, important contemporary issues related to economics, politics, social development, culture, and the environment were discussed from ethical and moral viewpoints, which increased awareness of the importance of responsible citizenship and professional values. The course also strengthened critical thinking, communication, and teamwork skills through essay assignments, group presentations, discussions, and creative activities. In addition, exposure to topics related to cultural diversity, national unity, and ethical practices enhanced appreciation for social responsibility and intercultural understanding in a multicultural society. Overall, this course successfully developed ethical awareness, civic-minded thinking, and interpersonal skills that are important for both personal and professional development.
